Welcome

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the School of Computer Science and Technology, China University of Mining and Technology, China. I received the M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from China University of Mining and Technology, Xuzhou, China, in 2015 and 2020, respectively. My research interests are primarily on image aesthetic quality assessment and affective computing.
